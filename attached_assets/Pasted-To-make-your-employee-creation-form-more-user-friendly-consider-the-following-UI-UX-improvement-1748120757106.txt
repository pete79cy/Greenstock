To make your **employee creation form** more user-friendly, consider the following UI/UX improvements:

---

### ✅ Layout & Visual Enhancements

#### 1. **Group Fields Visually**

Use cards or fieldsets with titles like:

* **Personal Details** (Full Name, Job Title)
* **Salary & Payment**
* **Identification**

#### 2. **Field Hints & Tooltips**

Add placeholder examples or `?` tooltips:

```html
<input placeholder="e.g., John Papadopoulos">
```

For example:

* Passport: `e.g., A26540193`
* ARC: `e.g., 581863693`
* Salary: show currency symbol with inline hint (`€/month`)

#### 3. **Inline Validation**

Show small, non-intrusive messages when a field is incomplete or malformed, e.g.:

```html
<div class="text-sm text-red-500 mt-1">Please enter a valid salary</div>
```

#### 4. **Autofocus & Tab Order**

Use `autofocus` on the first input, and ensure logical `tabindex` for keyboard navigation.
